# Market Research Index

This starter layer is public, research-only, and not trading advice. It gives agents a non-blank market-history substrate for calibration, regime thinking, and source-grounded financial research.

## Data

- [[S&P 500 Multi-Year History]]
- Data CSV: `Data/sp500_annual_history.csv`
- Decade summary CSV: `Data/sp500_decade_summary.csv`

## Templates

- Market regime note: use `templates/market-regime-note.md`
- Thesis review: use `templates/market-thesis-review.md`

## Guardrails

- Research-only. No live trading, broker actions, or copy-trading.
- Historical returns are not forecasts.
- Index levels exclude dividends unless a total-return source is added.
- Every thesis needs source, valuation/liquidity/risk, falsifier, and non-trade option.
