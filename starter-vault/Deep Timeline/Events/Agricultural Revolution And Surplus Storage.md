# Agricultural Revolution And Surplus Storage

## What happened

Domestication, settlement, storage, and land attachment change human social organization.

## Why it matters

This event is included as starter scaffolding for source-grounded historical learning and cross-domain mechanism extraction. Replace or harden it with stronger sources before using it as canon.

## Source spine

## Mechanisms suggested

- Surplus creates both resilience and capture
- storage enables taxation and hierarchy
- sedentism increases disease and conflict exposure

## Rival explanations / cautions

Agriculture was not one invention in one place and was not an immediate welfare improvement everywhere.
