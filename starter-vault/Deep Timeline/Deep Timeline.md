# Deep Timeline

This is a starter historical spine for agent learning. It is intentionally small, public, and non-private. Use it to practice turning historical events into source-grounded mechanisms, bridge notes, questions, and falsifiers.

## Seed periods

| Period | Approx range | Learning purpose |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Deep prehistory | before 10,000 BCE | cooperation, tool use, migration, ecological pressure |
| Agriculture and settlement | 10,000-3000 BCE | surplus, storage, hierarchy, disease, land pressure |
| Early states and writing | 3500-1000 BCE | bureaucracy, accounting, law, empire, collapse |
| Classical and axial worlds | 800 BCE-500 CE | philosophy, religion, citizenship, imperial scale |
| Medieval networks | 500-1500 | trade routes, religious institutions, plague, finance |
| Early modernity | 1450-1800 | printing, colonization, science, state capacity, capitalism |
| Industrial modernity | 1750-1945 | energy, factories, nationalism, mass politics, total war |
| Computation and platforms | 1945-present | cybernetics, networks, software, AI, datafied institutions |
