# Philosophy

The goal is not to remember everything. The goal is to improve judgment and future behavior.

Raw information becomes intelligence only when it is routed, tested, compressed, and made actionable. A note is not progress unless it changes a decision, artifact, rule, forecast, checklist, skill, or future question.

The north-star metric is:

```text
verified, reviewable, promoted-or-killed artifacts per unit of human attention and frontier-token spend
```

Use broad generation in sandboxed layers. Canonize narrowly through source grounding, verification, scored outcomes, and human review.
