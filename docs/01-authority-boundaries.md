# Authority Boundaries

Every layer has a job and a limit.

| Layer | Job | Cannot do by itself | Selection pressure |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Obsidian/Git | durable truth, source history, recovery | prove claims without evidence | source quality, links, diffs, log entries |
| Raw clippings | evidence/provenance | become canon automatically | hash logs, disposition labels |
| Skills | compressed operating procedure | store session diaries | future behavior change |
| Chat/messaging | workbench/cockpit | act as machine source of truth | compact summaries, evidence handles |
| Scripts/ledgers | deterministic state/checks | substitute for judgment | parse/schema/test results |
| Sandbox vault | fast mutation and pressure testing | canonize itself | promotion packets, decay reports |
| Agents/workers | execute/review/compress | self-certify final truth | handles, tests, verification |

Every recurring system should have a source-of-truth map: human source, machine source, derived artifacts, allowed writers, verification command, and rollback path.
