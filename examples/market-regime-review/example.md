# Example: Market Regime Review

Question: Was a bad index year just “the market went down,” or a specific regime?

Process:

1. Read `starter-vault/Market Research/Data/sp500_annual_history.csv`.
2. Identify the year return and within-year drawdown.
3. Add external source context: rates, inflation, earnings, credit, war/shock, policy.
4. Classify the regime with a falsifier.
5. Record a non-trade lesson.

Example conclusion shape:

A drawdown year with rising rates and contracting valuation multiples should not be analyzed the same way as an earnings-collapse recession or a sudden exogenous liquidity shock. The index return is the symptom; the regime note should name the transmission channel.
